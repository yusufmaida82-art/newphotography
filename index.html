About Me

madish

I am a passionate photographer based in NEWYORK CITY. My love for photography started at a young age when I picked up my first camera and discovered the joy of freezing time through a single frame.

I specialize in landscape and portrait photography, always chasing the perfect light. Every photo I take is a reflection of how I see the world — full of beauty in the smallest details.

When I am not behind the lens, I enjoy hiking, traveling, and exploring new places that inspire my next shot.
